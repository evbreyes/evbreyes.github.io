Greetings, I'm Elmar!

User Experience Designer

I am a User Experience Designer studying Human-Computer Interaction at DePaul University in Chicago. I have over 5 years experience practicing quantitative data analysis for chemical processes in the chemical and aerospace field. I enjoy empathizing and learning about the values, thoughts and ideas of people.

Some skills I’ve recently picked up from my courses include, but not limited to, conducting user interviews and contextual inquiries, usability testing, persona creation, journey mapping, Information Architecture and rapid prototyping.

Recent Projects

Virtual Vet Clinic App

An app design for parents who struggle with vet visits

Course Project - DePaul University | 9/14/20 to 11/21/20

My Virtual Vet Clinic is a mobile application focused on pet medical health. There are many pet owners that face different struggles, such as making vet appointments, taking their pet to the clinic, or simply finding a vet that fulfills their needs. The My Virtual Vet Clinic app solves this problem by making pet care more accessible.
