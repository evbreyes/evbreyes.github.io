About Me!

Elmar Von Brian M. Reyes

User Experience Designer

I am a collaborative User Experience Designer studying Human-Computer Interaction at DePaul University in Chicago. Although I am in the Master’s program at Chicago, I am currently based at Indianapolis, IN. I have more than 5 years of experience practicing quantitative data analyses through my chemical and aerospace background. I closely work with a group of multi-disciplinary individuals ensuring that our client and stakeholders are satisfied with the quick delivery of analysis results.

I believe continuous improvement and innovation across all levels of the organization is important. I am also very enthusiastic about positive and engaging team cultures.

Additionally, empathy and learning to understand our users are important skills I can provide as a UX research team member.
