Usage & Best Practices

  • Cards are used on the home page and projects page of the portfolio.
  • A card must include a photo, project title, subtitle, and description of the project linked to the card.
  • It is recommended to use a darker background to improve contrast between the background and the card.
  • In large and XL screens, the cards alternate between the regular positioned and reversed cards.

Accessibility

  • Must have enough contrast for text and background to ensure easy readability.
  • The button or call to action must contain either an accent color or contrasting color to improve finability.
