Usage & Best Practices

  • The buttons are call to actions used in forms or cards to specify an action.
  • Button text should be in verb form to indicate an action.

Accessibility

  • Ensure the there is sufficient contrast between the background and text colors.
  • Use descriptive button labels, preferrably verbs.
