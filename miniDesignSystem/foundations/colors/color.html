My Color Scheme

Below is the color palette chosen for my website. I chose a base color of purple and picked a mixture of monochromatic and split complementary. In color psychology, Purple is often associated with royalty, wisdom, creativity, and curiosity. For my website, I am using it to communicate that I am a curious and creative leader.
