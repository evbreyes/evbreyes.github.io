A Mini Design System

By Elmar Reyes

Introduction

Welcome to my newly formed mini design system!

Welcome to the mini design system I will be using for my UX professional portfolio. Within this website, you will find three categories of design guidelines and patterns. These three categories are:

  • Foundations - Includes the basic components for colours, logos, and typography.
  • Components - Includes the building blocks of my portfolio from header, footer, cards, and button guidelines.
  • Patterns - Includes example pages on how to use the Foundations and Components.
A bit about me...

Ever since high school, I've identified myself as a leader. I also think of myself as reliable, creative, and an advocate for continuous growth and learning. As someone transitioning into the User Experience field, these are just a few attributes I can contribute to the UX world. The main color scheme I chose relies heavily on the color purple, which represents both royalty and creativity, to communicate that I am a leader that values innovation.
