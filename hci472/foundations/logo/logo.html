Logo Designs

My logo encorporates my name's first and last initial, ER. This page explains the different styles and uses of the logo. Different variations include a colored logo, grayscale logo, inverted color and grayscale logo, header or navigation logo, and favicon.

Colored Logo

This logo is the official colored logo. It is used on the website or other publications that require a light background.

Grayscale Logo

This is the grayscale logo that can be used for publications that require black and white print. It is used on a white or light colored background.

Inverted Colored Logo

This logo is specifically made for black or darker backgrounds. The colors from the colored logo were inverted to emphasize contrast on the letter R.

Inverted Grayscale Logo

This is an inverted grayscale logo to be used on darker backgrounds with the absence of color.

Navigation Header Logo

This logo is used for the navigation panel. It is a horizontally rectangular shape that helps ensure the navigation bar does not become too big.

Favicon

This is the favicon to be used for my website. It is meant to be a smaller version of my colored logo.
